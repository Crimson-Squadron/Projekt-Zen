Isle of Berk

Where Dragons Soar and Advanture Awaits!

EXPLORE

Chapter 01

Discovery of Berk Island

Berk Island was discovered by a group of Viking sailors who were stranded in the middle of the sea during a fierce storm. They stumbled upon the island by chance while seeking refuge from the storm. Although the island initially seemed unwelcoming, they soon realized its incredible potential.

Chapter 02

Meeting with a Dragon

One of the expedition members, a young sailor named Hiccup, accidentally encountered a wounded dragon in the island's forest. Despite his initial fear, Hiccup decided to help the dragon, whom he later named Toothless. Their bond grew stronger over time, and Hiccup learned a great deal about dragons from Toothless.

Chapter 03

Establishing the Academy

Seeing the potential for coexistence with dragons, Hiccup and a small group of villagers came together to form the Dragon Training Academy. They believed that with a better understanding of dragons, they could live alongside these creatures without conflict.

Chapter 04

Conflict with an Evil Dragon

However, not all dragons were willing to make peace with humans. As the Academy was being established, an evil dragon emerged, threatening the peace of Berk Island. Hiccup and the Academy students had to unite to fight this menace and protect their island from danger.

Chapter 05

Recovery and Peace

With courage and teamwork, Hiccup and the Dragon Training Academy students managed to defeat the evil dragon and restore peace to Berk Island. This experience taught them that friendship and understanding are key to living harmoniously with other creatures, including dragons. Over time, the Dragon Training Academy grew into an important learning center for the residents of Berk Island and the surrounding dragons. The relationship between humans and dragons became stronger, bringing peace and prosperity to the island.
