"For King and Country"

Zen Dragon Academy is the leader in educating and training the world's best dragon trainers in utilizing and working together with their dragons to preserve the peace in the world. Nestled within the serene peaks of the Dragon's Spine Mountains, Zen Dragon Academy stands as the pinnacle of excellence in the realm of dragon training. For centuries, it has been the crucible where aspiring dragon trainers hone their skills, guided by ancient wisdom and modern techniques. The academy's curriculum transcends mere instruction; it instills in its students a profound understanding of the mystical bond between human and dragon. Here, students learn not only the art of dragon riding and combat but also the intricacies of communication, empathy, and mutual respect with their scaled companions.
